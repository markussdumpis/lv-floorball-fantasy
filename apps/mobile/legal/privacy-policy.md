# Privacy Policy  
Last updated: February 2026

Welcome to Latvian Floorball Fantasy ("we", "our", "us").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use our app, we may collect the following information:

### Account Information
- Email address (used for authentication)
- User ID (generated by Supabase)
- Nickname

### Gameplay Data
- Selected players
- Team compositions
- Captain selections
- Fantasy points
- Gameweek activity
- Leaderboard rankings

### Technical Information
- Device type
- App version
- Basic analytics (if implemented)

We do NOT collect sensitive personal data such as payment information.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Authenticate and manage your account
- Display your team and leaderboard rankings
- Calculate fantasy points
- Improve app functionality
- Maintain fair competition

---

## 3. Data Storage

All data is securely stored using Supabase infrastructure.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data.

We may share data only with essential service providers:
- Supabase (authentication & database hosting)
- App infrastructure providers (hosting services)

---

## 5. Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as your account is active.

If you delete your account, all associated personal data will be permanently removed from our database.

---

## 6. Account Deletion

You can request account deletion directly inside the app in the Profile/Settings section.

Once deleted:
- Your profile
- Fantasy teams
- Points
- Leaderboard history

will be permanently removed.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Correct your nickname
- Delete your account at any time
